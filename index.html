Biography

Mohit has joined the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, National Institute of Technology Hamirpur in June, 2020. He did his Masters (M. Tech.) in Information Technology from Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad. He received his PhD in 2018 from Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati on "Automatic Speaker Recognition using Low Resources: Experiments in Feature Reduction and Learning" under the supervision of Prof. Pradip K Das. The focus of the work was to reduce the features used for automatic speaker recognition and to learn a smaller feature set for classification of different speakers. Prior to joining NIT Hamirpur, he served as Assistant Professor (Senior Grade) in the Department of CSE/IT, JIIT Noida. Before that he served as Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, IIIT Bhagalpur. His current research areas of interest include Speech Processing, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing and Image Processing applications.
